Unified Decision-Support Software Tools

Mission

We developed, tested and maintained Unified Decision-Support Software Tools.

The system is primarily developed for the financial decision-making process. The tools are based on extensive research in statistical and mathematical optimization models.

At the moment the application of said tools has become wider in such areas as military and medicine.

Technologies

C/C++, Fortran, Mathematica, Matlab, SQL, PSG, MFC, STL, NAG, .NET Framework, WinSCP
